People killed by the police

This is a visualisation about people killed by the police in the United States during the years 2013 and 2014. Explore the data by hovering your mouse over different counties. The map that you see now shows the amounts of people killed by the police in different counties, but you can change the variable to visualise the people killed by age, gender and race as well. When you change the variable, give the map a few seconds to reload.

You can explore the data by hovering your mouse over different counties and changing the variable to visualise by. The data is complete from the years 2013 and 2014. Please note, that the categorisation by race may be biased, since in many cases the race of the deceased is not reported.

This visualization was made for the course ME-E4500 - Explorative Information Visualization in Aalto University.
